What's EuRuKo?

EuRuKo is an annual conference about the Ruby language with an informal atmosphere and lots of opportunities to listen, to talk, to hack and to have fun. This year takes place in Krakow, Poland, on the 29th-30th of May.

Registration is now open closed!

Despite having more seats available than any past EuRuKo conference, EuRuKo 2010 sold out in 22 hours. 

Register now for 40 Euro plus VAT and guarantee your place at EuRuKo 2010!

The registration is held by Ekobilet, an environmentally-friendly alternative to traditional tickets. You can present your Ekobilet ticket on your mobile device at check in rather than printing it.

Using 2D codes to automate checkin takes all the pain out of the conference check in process.
